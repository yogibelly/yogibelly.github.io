This Is How We Eat

Homemade Healthy Vegetarian Indian Food

We love butter chicken from Indian restaurants too, but this is about something different: our priority is on healthy Indian food you could eat everyday. You will be surprised to see how much more there is to Indian cuisine than what you find at Indian restaurants. We hope to help you discover how people really eat in a culture rooted in the longest tradition of vegetarian cooking.

Let us take care of Meatless Mondays

Are you running out of ideas for what to cook when trying to eat less meat? With YogiBelly you will get to try a variety of new vegetarian dishes: pumpkin, black chick peas, squash, stuffed mini-eggplant, edo, lotus root, bitter melon, lentil kebabs in tart tumeric curry,... and more. All our food is 100% vegetarian (no eggs either). Many of our dishes are also vegan. (caveat: we only serve Tuesdays and Wednesdays for now)

Our Commitment to Sustainability

We deliver all our food in reusable glass jars and bags so you don't have to feel guilty about the packaging. We also compost all kitchen trimmings. Please help us keep to our commitment to sustainability: Keep your jars and bag. We will take them back upon delivery of your next order!

Delivery

We offer delivery to the Plateau, Mile-End and Outremont. A surcharge may apply for delivery outside these areas.
