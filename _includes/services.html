Homemade Indian Vegetarian Food Inspired by Ayurvedic Principles

Personal Indian Catering Service

We specialize in Indian food that you won't find at a typical Indian restaurant: pumpkin, black chick peas, squash, stuffed mini-eggplant, edo, lotus root, bitter melon, lentil kebabs in tart tumeric curry and more.

Healthy Vegetarian Fare

We have always struggled in getting our kids to eat a varied healthy diet, that wasn't just chicken and bagels. What's more, we always strive to reduce our consumption of meat, but are often stuck in our meal planning with the North American tradition of a main course of meat, surrounded by some vegetables...or pasta. All our food is 100% vegetarian (no eggs either). Many of our dishes are also vegan.

Insipred by Ayurvedic Principles

Although Ayurvedic principles are ingrained in Indian cuisine, most of the food served in Indian restaurants does not represent this ancient system of well-being. Our food is inspired by Ayurveda (Ayur = Life, Veda = Science). The most important principle in the Ayurvedic Diet is that your food is fresh, seasonal, and as often as possible local. Fresh doesn't, however, mean raw. The best are freshly cooked, whole meals.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

We deliver all our food in reusable glass MASON jars so you don't have to feel guilty about the packaging.
